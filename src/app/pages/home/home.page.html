Supply from Exarep is shown alongside pass-through delivery charges from your TDU—typically volumetric (per kWh) and monthly base amounts approved by regulators.

How Texas electricity pricing works

In ERCOT’s competitive retail market, your retail electric provider sells electricity supply; your TDU delivers it and bills approved delivery charges. Your total bill includes supply, delivery, and taxes or fees as applicable. Actual charges depend on your usage, plan documents, and current tariffs—review your Electricity Facts Label (EFL) and Terms of Service before enrolling.
