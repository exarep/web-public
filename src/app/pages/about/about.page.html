About Exarep

Our Mission

Exarep was founded with a simple belief: Texans deserve an electricity provider that is honest about pricing, responsive when they need help, and committed to delivering reliable power.

As a licensed Retail Electric Provider in the deregulated Texas market, we purchase wholesale electricity and sell it directly to homes and businesses across the state. We work with your local Transmission and Distribution Service Provider (TDSP) to ensure seamless delivery.

We believe the deregulated market should empower consumers with real choices — not confuse them with misleading rates and hidden fees. Every plan we offer comes with a clear Electricity Facts Label so you know exactly what you are paying.

How the Texas Electricity Market Works

Deregulation

Texas deregulated its electricity market in 2002, giving consumers the power to choose their Retail Electric Provider. The generation, transmission, distribution, and retail sale of electricity are handled by separate entities.

Your TDSP

Your Transmission and Distribution Service Provider (also called a TDU) owns the poles, wires, and meters that deliver electricity to your home. Regardless of which REP you choose, your TDSP remains the same and delivery charges are regulated.

ERCOT

The Electric Reliability Council of Texas manages the flow of electricity across the Texas grid and facilitates the retail market, including customer switching between REPs.

Seamless Switching

When you switch to Exarep, we submit an enrollment request through ERCOT. Your TDSP handles the transition and your power stays on the entire time. There is no interruption in service.
